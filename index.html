Let's play a game.

Remember Me is a game to train your brain.
The computer generates a pattern, you as a player
need to replicate this pattern.

After you see the computer pattern you can use your mouse
to click the blinking buttons, or you can use the arrow keys.
You can click start in the middle to start the game.

Make sure you follow the instructions in the middle.

If you want to stop the music click the speaker icon at the bottom.

Now let's start playing by clicking the button below.
Have fun!
